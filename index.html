About

The amount of people eating a plant based diet is increasing, which is a fantastic! Industrial agriculture is one of the leading contributors to Co2 omissions, and reducing the consumption of animal based products is the single most effective way an individual can reduce their own carbon footprint. Vegan Meets aims to keep this momentum going with people sharing their fun, ethical and tasty adventures!
